About IU

IU (아이유), born Lee Ji-eun on May 16, 1993, is a South Korean singer-songwriter and actress. She signed with Kakao Entertainment (formerly LOEN Entertainment) in 2007 and debuted in 2008 at the age of 15.

Known as the "Nation's Little Sister," IU has become one of the most influential and best-selling solo artists in South Korea. Her musical style spans various genres including ballads, pop, jazz, and folk.
